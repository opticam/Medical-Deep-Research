# Medical Deep Research Agent

Google Project ID: `agent-orchestration-500407`

Medical Deep Research is a focused project for running evidence-driven medical investigations with **agent orchestration**.  
It coordinates multiple AI agents to break complex clinical or scientific questions into manageable tasks, gather findings, and synthesize clear outputs for review.

## How agent orchestration is used

- **Task decomposition:** A coordinator agent splits a broad research question into smaller investigation threads.
- **Parallel evidence gathering:** Specialized agents independently search literature, extract key details, and surface supporting/contradicting data.
- **Structured synthesis:** A synthesis stage combines findings into concise summaries with transparent reasoning trails.
- **Iterative refinement:** Agents can be re-run with narrower prompts to deepen analysis or resolve conflicting evidence.

## How medical professionals can use this project

Medical professionals can use Medical Deep Research to support high-depth research workflows such as:

- Reviewing emerging evidence for specific conditions, therapies, or diagnostics
- Comparing treatment pathways and identifying evidence gaps
- Preparing structured background briefs for clinical meetings or case discussions
- Accelerating literature triage before deeper manual appraisal

## Practical workflow for deep research

1. Define a focused clinical or biomedical question.
2. Run orchestrated agents to collect and organize relevant evidence.
3. Review synthesized outputs, then request deeper follow-up on unresolved points.
4. Validate final conclusions with professional judgment and local clinical standards.

## Important note

This project is intended to **assist** research and analysis.  
It does not replace clinician judgment, institutional protocols, or formal medical guidance.
